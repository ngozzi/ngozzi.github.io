Manipulation in a Simple Market

  • Agent-based simulation of the stock market

The aim of this work is to analyze the behavior of trading agents in a simple stock market in which benchmarks for risk exposure (in our case an index that estimates volatility) are manipulated. The idea comes from recent events: at the beginning of February 2018, large fluctuations of the Cboe Volatility Index (VIX), whose aim is to estimate the implied volatility of S&P500 stocks, caused a flash-crash in the US stock market.
